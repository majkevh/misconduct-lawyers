THIS MATTER is before the undersigned Chairperson of the Grievance Committee of
the N.C. State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter IB, Section .Ol18(g).
Katherine E. Jean represents the North Carolina State Bar and Bryant D. Webster
represents Elizabeth Spradlin. Based upon facts presented and with the consent of the
member and of the counsel, the undersigned makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
]. The North Carolina State Bar is a body duly organized under the laws of North
Carolina and is the proper party to consent to this proceeding under the authority granted
it in Chapter 84 of the General Statutes of North Carolina and the Rules and Regulations
of the North Carolina State Bar promulgated thereunder.
2. Elizabeth Spradlin was admitted to practice law in North Carolina on August 20, 1993
and is an Attorney at Law licensed to practice in North Carolina, subject to the rules,
regulations and Revised Rules of Professional Conduct of the North Carolina State Bar
and the laws of the State of North Carolina.
3. Elizabeth Spradlin actively engaged in the practice of law in the State of North
Carolina and maintained a law office in Buncombe County, North Carolina.
4. Elizabeth Spradlin currently suffers from bipolar disorder.
S. The above condition significantly impairs Elizabeth Spradlin's professional judgment,
performance or competence as an attorney.
6. Elizabeth Spradlin consents to being transferred to disability inactive status.

BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact, the undersigned makes the following:
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1. Pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code IB Section .Ol18(g), the proper parties have
consented to the transfer of Elizabeth Spradlin to disability inactive status.
2. Elizabeth Spradlin is disabled within the meaning of 27 N.C. Admin. Code 1B Section
.0 103(18).
THEREFORE, it is hereby ORDERED:

I. Elizabeth Spradlin is transferred to disability inactive status.
2. Elizabeth Spradlin shall refrain from practicing law in North Carolina until reinstated

to active status pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter IB Section .0125(c).
