DISBARMENT
JAMES T. CONRAD

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of
the North Carolina State Bar pursuant to Section .0017 of Subchapter B of
the Rules and Regulations of the North Carolina State Bar upon an affidavit
of tender oflicense executed by James T. Conrad and filed in the offices of
the North Carolina State Bar on October 13,2006.
Based upon the affidavit of tender oflicense, the Council finds that James T.
Conrad misappropriated at least $314,000.00 of entrusted funds for his own
personal use. The entrusted funds were held by Conrad as trustee for a
family trust of which Conrad and his siblings were intended beneficiaries.
The Council also finds that James T. Conrad has waived his right to notice
of this hearing and the right to be heard before the Council. The Council
concludes that the conduct of James T. Conrad constitutes grounds for
discipline under N.C. Gen. Stat. §84-28(b)(2) in that James T. Conrad
violated Rules 8.4(a), (b) and (c) of the Revised Rules of Professional
Conduct and that the Council may enter an order of discipline accordingly.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar enters the following order:
1. The tender of surrender of license 00 ames T. Conrad is hereby accepted.
2. James T. Conrad is hereby DISBARRED from the practice of law in
North Carolina.

3. James T. Conrad will comply with the provisions of Subchapter B,
Section .0024 of the Rules and Regulations of the North Carolina State Bar
and will surrender his license and permanent registration card to the
Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar along with the affidavit required
by Subchapter B, Section .0024.
4. The costs of this action are taxed against James T. Conrad.
