IN THE MATTER OF
THE REINSTATEMENT OF
ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT
MARK L. BIBBS

THIS MATTER is before the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to a Petition for Reinstatement filed by Mark L. Bibbs ("Bibbs") on December 2, 2013.
Based upon the petition and a review of the records of the North Carolina State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1.
Bibbs was suspended from the practice of law for a period of one year effective
January 2, 2013.
2.
Pursuant to the terms of the Order, after completion of three months of an active
suspension of his license, Bibbs was eligible to apply for a stay of the balance of the
suspension upon the filing of a verified petition with the Secretary of the North Carolina
State Bar at least thirty days before any proposed effective date ofthe stay.
3.
One year has elapsed since the effective date of Petitioner's suspension and
Petitioner did not request a stay of the balance of the one year active suspension after
serving a three month active suspension, but rather chose to serve the full one year active
suspension.
4.
On December 2, 2013, Bibbs filed a petition for reinstatement In his petition,
Bibbs described the actions he has taken to comply with the requirements set out in 27
NCAC lB, Sec. 0125(b).
5.
Counsel for the North Carolina State Bar did not file an objection to Bibbs'
petition.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact, and without any evidence
presented in opposition to the petition, the Secretary makes the following

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1. The petitioner, Mark 1. Bibbs, has satisfied the requirements set out in 27
NCAC lB, Sec .. 0 12S(b) and is entitled to reinstatement of his license to practice law in
this jurisdiction.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, the
Secretary hereby enters the following:
ORDER
Mark 1. Bibbs is hereby reinstated to the active practice of law in North Carolina
as of the date of this order.
