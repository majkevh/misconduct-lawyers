ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B, §
.0117(a) of the State Bar Discipline & Disability Rules upon an affidavit of tender of
surrender of license executed by Paul W. White on October 16th, 2000 and filed in the
offices of the North Carolina State Bar on October 16,2000.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that Paul W. White's conduct of
misappropriating client funds is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. § 84­
28(b)(2) in that Paul W. White violated Rules 1.2(b)( and (c) of the Rules of Professional
Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon Motion made and duly seconded, the Council enters the
following Order:
1. The tender of surrender of license of Paul W. White is hereby accepted.
2. Paul W. White is hereby is DISBARRED from the practice oflaw in North
Carolina.
3. Paul W. White shall comply with the provisions of 27 N.C.A.C. Chapter L
Subchapter B, § .0124 of the State Bar Discipline & Disbarment Rules and shall
surrender his license and permanent membership card to the Secretary of the North
Carolina State Bar along with the affidavit required by § .0124 of the State Bar Discipline
& Disbarment Rules.
4. The costs of this action are taxed against Paul W. White.
Done and ordered this 20'h day of October 2000.
