ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MADER was heard by the Council of the North Carolina State Bar
pursuant to 27 N"CAC. 1B §.0117 (a) of the State Bar Discipline and Disability Rules
upon the affidavit of tender of surrender of license executed by David Curtis Smith on
April 20, 2009, and filed in the offices of the State Bar on April 21, 2009.
Based on the affidavit, the Council finds that David Curtis Smith misappropriated
at least $227,082.45 from his attorney trust account that had been entrusted to him in a
fiduciary capacity, at least $200,000.00 of which he utilized for his own benefit without
authorization from the beneficial owners of the funds. This conduct constitutes
grounds for discipline, under N.C. Gen. Stat. § 84-28 (b)(2), in that Smith violated Rule
1.15-20) and Rule 8.4 of the Revised Rules of Professional Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion made and duly seconded, the Council enters the
following Order:
1. The tender of surrender of the license of David Curtis Smith is hereby
accepted.
2. David Curtis Smith hereby is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North
Carolina.
3. David Curtis Smith shall comply with the provisions of 27 NCAC 1B § .0124 of
the State Bar Discipline and Disability Rules and shall surrender his license and
permanent membership card to the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar
along with the affidavit required by 27 NCAC 1B § .0124 of the State Bar
Discipline and Disability Rules.
4. The costs of this action are taxed against David Curtis Smith .
