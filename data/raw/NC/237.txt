ORDER OF DISBARMENT

This matter coming on to be heard and being heard on July 21,
1995 before the Council of the North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to Title 27, N.C.A.C., Chapter 1, Subchapter B, section .0117
upon the tender of surrender of license of Thomas M. Wade, Jr.
made by affidavit sworn to and subscribed on June 29, 1995 and
filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on June 29,
1995; and
BASED UPON the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct
of Thomas M. Wade, Jr. is grounds for discipline pursuant to
N.C.G.S. Sec. 84-28(b) (2) in that Thomas M. Wade, Jr.
misappropriated clients' funds in violation of Rules 1.2(b) & (c)
and 10.1 (a) & (c) of the NC Rules of Professional Conduct found
in Title 27, N.C.A.C., Chapter 2.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council
enters the following ORDER:
1.	

The tender of surrender of license of Thomas M. Wade, Jr.
is hereby accepted.

2.	

THOMAS M. WADE, JR. is hereby DISBARRED from the practice
of law in North Carolina.

3.	

Thomas M. Wade, Jr. shall forthwith surrender his license
certificate and membership card to the Secretary.

4.	

Thomas M. Wade, Jr. shall comply with the provisions of
Title 27, N.C.A.C., Chapter 1, subchapter B, Section
.0124 within the time limits contained in that section.

5.	

Thomas M. Wade, Jr. is taxed with the costs of this
action as assessed by the Secretary.
