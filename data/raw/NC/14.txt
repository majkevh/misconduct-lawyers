ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the North
Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter I, Subchapter B, §.0117(a) of the
State Bar Discipline & Disability Rules upon an affidavit oftender of surrender of license
executed by R. Lewis Alexander, Jr. on January 11,2001 and filed in the offices of the North
Carolina State Bar on January 18, 200 I.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council fmds that R. Lewis Alexander, Jr.'s conduct of
misappropriating client funds is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. §84-28(b)(2)
in that R. Lewis Alexander, Jr. violated Rules 8.4(b) and (c) of the Revised Rules of Professional
Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the following
Order:
I. The tender of surrender of license of R. Lewis Alexander, Jr. is hereby accepted.
2. R. Lewis Alexander, Jr. is hereby is DISBARRED from the practice oflaw in North
Carolina.
3. R. Lewis Alexander, Jr. shall comply with the provisions of27 N.C.A.C. Chapter I,
Subchapter B, §.0124 of the State Bar Discipline & Disbarment Rules and shall surrender his
license and permanent membership card to the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar along
with the affidavit required by § .0124 of the State Bar Discipline & Disbarment Rules.
4. The costs of this action are taxed against R. Lewis Alexander, Jr.
Done and ordered this 19 th day of January, 2001.

AFFIDAVIT

To: The Council of the North Carolina State Bar:
1. I, R. Lewis Alexander, Jr., hereby tender my law license and desire to resign from the
North Carolina State Bar. My resignation is freely and voluntarily rendered. It is not the result
of coercion or duress. I am fully aware of the implications of submitting my resignation.
2. I am aware that there is pending an investigation or other proceeding regarding
allegations that I misappropriated funds from my law firm's trust account.
3. I acknowledge that the material facts forming the basis of the investigation or other
proceeding and referenced herein this affidavit are true.
4. I submit my resignation because I know if the State Bar proceedings were predicated
upon the misconduct under investigation, I could not successfully defend against them.
5. I hereby waive notice of and consent to the council considering this affidavit
surrendering my license at the meeting on Friday, January 19,2001.
