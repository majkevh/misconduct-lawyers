tender of surrender of the law license of Adam Loren Baker. Petitioner, the North Carolina

Schneider.

Loren Baker and the consent of the parties, the Court makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1.

IT IS NOW, THEREFORE, ORDERED:

1.

The surrender of the license of Adam Loren Baker is hereby accepted.

2.
Adam Loren Baker is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North Carolina
effective upon entry of this Order.
3.
Baker shall comply with the provisions of27 N.C. Admin. Code 1B ยง.0124 and
shall surrender his law license and permanent membership card to the Secretary of the North
Carolina State Bar along with the affidavit required by Rule .0124.

4.
Within 15 days of the effective date of this Order Baker shall provide the State
Bar with an address and telephone number at which clients seeking return of files can
communicate with Baker and obtain such files.
5.
Baker shall promptly retum client files in his possession, custody, or control to
clients upon request, within five days of receipt of such request. Baker will be deemed to have
received any such request three days after the date the request is sent to him if the request is
sent to the address Baker provided to the State Bar pursuant to this Order.

6.
Baker shall not petition for reinstatement of his law license until the expiration
of at least five years from the date of entry of this order.
7.
Baker's law license shall not be reinstated until he has demonstrated to the
satisfaction of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission and the Council of the North Carolina
State Bar that he has satisfied the conditions for reinstatement set out in 27 N.C. Admin. Code
