This matter is before the undersigned Chairperson of the North Carolina State
Bar's Grievance Committee pursuant to 27 NCAC lB, §.0118(g); with A. Root
Edmonson representing the North Carolina State Bar and Paul M. Sylvia representing
himself. Based upon the information provided by Paul M. Sylvia, and with the consent of
Paul M. Sylvia and deputy counsel, the undersigned makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1. The North Carolina State Bar is a body duly organized under the laws of North
Carolina and is the proper party to consent to this order under the authority granted it in
Chapter 84 of the General Statutes of North Carolina, and the Rules and Regulations of
the North Carolina State Bar promulgated thereunder.
2. Paul M. Sylvia ("Sylvia") was admitted to the North Carolina State Bar on
August 21, 2001 and is an Attorney at Law licensed to practice law in North Carolina,
subject to the rules, regulations, and the Rules of Professional Conduct of the North
Carolina State Bar and the laws of North Carolina.
3. Sylvia previously engaged in the practice oflaw in the State of North Carolina,
and maintained a law office in the city of Wilmington, New Hanover County, North
Carolina.
4. Sylvia presently suffers from depression that significantly impairs his
professional judgment, performance or competence as an attorney.
