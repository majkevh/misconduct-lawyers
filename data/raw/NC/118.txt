ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT

THIS MATTER is before the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to a Petition for Reinstatement filed by Melvin L. Wall, Jr. ("Wall") on December 21,
2007.
Based upon the petition and a review of the records of the North Carolina State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1. On June 10,2004, a hearing committee of the Disciplinary Hearing
Commission CDHC") entered an Order of Discipline in 03 DHC 11 ("Order of
Discipline") suspending Wall from the practice oflaw for three years with the possibility
of thirty months of the suspension being stayed if Wall complied with certain conditions.
2. On December 21,2007, Wall filed a petition for reinstatement showing that he
had satisfied the conditions of the Order of Discipline for reinstatement after expiration
of the suspension.
3. Counsel for the North Carolina State Bar did not file an objection to Wall's
petition.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact, the Secretary makes the following
CONCLUSION OF LA W
1. The Petitioner, Melvin L. Wall, Jr., has satisfied the requirements set out
in 27 NCAC IB, ยง .0125(b) and is entitled to reinstatement of his license to practice law
in this jurisdiction.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, the
Secretary enters the following:

ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT
Melvin L. Wall is hereby reinstated to the active practice oflaw in North Carolina
as of the date afthis order.
