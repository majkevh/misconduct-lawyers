THIS MATTER came on to be heard and was heard by the undersigned
Chairperson of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission ("DHC") pursuant to 27 N.C.
Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B, Section .0117(d) of the North Carolina State Bar
Discipline & Disability Rules. Petitioner, the North Carolina State Bar, was represented
by Barry S. McNeill. The Defendant, Jimmy A. Pettus ("Pettus" or "Defendant"),
appeared pro se.
Based upon the Affidavit of Surrender of Law License submitted to the DHC
Chairperson by Pettus and the consent of the parties, the undersigned makes the
following:
FINDINGS OF FACT

•

I.

Pettus was licensed to practice law on August 23, 1997.

2.
During all periods relevant hereto, Pettus was engaged in the practice of
law in the State of North Carolina and practiced in the law firm ofH. Russell Vick &
Associates, P.L.L.C. ("Vicle & Associates") in Greensboro, Guilford County, North
Carolina.
3.
Between November 2010 and December 2011, Pettus misappropriated
entlUsted federal and state tax withholdings and employees' Simple IRA contributions
("entlUsted funds") for his own benefit or for the benefit ofVick & Associates.
4.
Pettus's Affidavit of Surrender of Law License meets the requirements of
27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter I, SUbchapter B, Section .OI17(a)(l), (2), (3), and (4).
