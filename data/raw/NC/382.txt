CONSENT ORDER
OF DISBARMENT

THIS MATTER came on to be heard and was heard by the undersigned Judge of
the Superior Court of Wake County. Petitioner, the North Carolina State Bar, was
represented by Katherine E. Jean. Respondent, Joel H. Brewer, was represented by Alan
M. Schneider.
Based upon the Affidavit of Surrender of Law License submitted to the court by
Brewer, the court makes the following:

FINDINGS OF FACT
I. Respondent, Joel H. Brewer ("Brewer"), was licensed to practice law in North
Carolina on August 24, 1975.
2. At all times relevant hereto, Brewer was the elected district attomey of the 9A
Prosecutorial District.
3. Brewer pled guilty to and was convicted of seven counts of misdemeanor assault on a
female in violation of N.C. Gen Stat. §14-33(c)(2), one count of misdemeanor
impersonating a law enforcement officer in violation ofN.C. Gen Stat. §14-277(a)(2),
and one count of misdemeanor willful failure to discharge duties in violation of N.C. Gen
Stat. §14-230 in the District Court of Person County, files nos. 10 CR 0717 through 10
CR0725.
4. Brewer committed the conduct for which he was charged and convicted while he was
the elected district attomey of the 9A Prosecutorial District.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact, the court makes the following:

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

1. By committing the crimes of which he was convicted, Brewer violated Rule 8.4(b) of
the North Carolina Rules of Professional Conduct.
2. The crimes of which Brewer was convicted are crimes showing professional unfitness.
3. Brewer's misconduct constitutes grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat.
Section 84-28(b)(1).
IT IS NOW, THEREFORE, ORDERED:
1. Joel H. Brewer is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North Carolina.
2. Joel H. Brewer shall comply with the provisions of Subchapter B, Section.0124 ofthe
Rules and Regulations of the North Carolina State Bar and shall sun·ender his license and
penmll1ent membership card to the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar along with
the affidavit required by Subchapter B, Section .0124.
3. Administrative fees and costs of this action are taxed against Joel H. Brewer.
