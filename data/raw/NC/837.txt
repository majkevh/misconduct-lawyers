ORDER OF DISBARMENT

FREDDIE LANE, JR.

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the North
Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter 1, Subchapter B, § .01l7(a) of
the State Bar Discipline & Disability Rules upon tender of an affidavit of surrender of license
executed by Freddie Lane, Jr. ("F. Lane") on October 21,2014 and filed in the offices ofthe
North Carolina State Bar on October 21,2014.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that F. Lane misappropriated entrusted funds
and failed to file and pay federal and state income and withholding taxes, in violation of
Rules 8.4(b) and 8.4(c) of the Rules of Professional Conduct. F. Lane's conduct is grounds for
discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. § 84-28(b)(2).
WHEREFORE, upon motion made and duly seconded, the Council enters the following
Order:
1.

The tender of surrender of license of Freddie Lane, Jr. is hereby accepted.

2.

Freddie Lane, Jr. is hereby DISBARRED from the practice of law in North
Carolina.

3.

F. Lane shall comply with the provisions of27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter 1,
Subchapter B, § .0124 of the State Bar Discipline & Disability Rules. F. Lane
shall surrender his license and permanent membership card to the Secretary ofthe
North Carolina State Bar along with the affidavit required by § .0124 of the State
Bar Discipline & Disability Rules within ten (10) days after the expiration of the
wind-down period provided under § .0117(e).

4.

The administrative fees and costs of this action are taxed against F. Lane.

Done and ordered this 24th day of October, 2014.
