CONSENT ORDER OF
INTERIM SUSPENSION

This cause carne before the undersigned Chair of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission pursuant
to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter I, Suhchapter B, Rule .0115(d) of the Discipline & Disability
Rules of the North Carolina State Bar on petition for an order of interim suspension of Joseph
Lee Levinson's license to practice law in North Carolina based upon his guilty plea to crimes
showing professional unfitness in the United States District Court for the Middle District of
North Carolina. Based upon the petition and the certified copy of the guilty plea, the
undersigned hereby makes the following
FINDINGS OF FACT
I.
Joseph Lee Levinson was licensed to practice law in North Carolina on 30
January 2004 and is, and was at all times referred to herein, an attorney at law subject to the
rules, regulations, and Rules of Professional Conduct of the North Carolina State Bar and the
laws of North Carolina.
2.
The current address of record for Levinson with the North Carolina State Bar is
P.O. Box /17, Benson, North Carolina, 27504.
3.
Levinson was charged with two felony counts in United States v. Joseph Lee
Levinson, M.D.N.C. case #1: 15-CR-413-1.
4.
On 8 January 2016, Levinson pled guilty to Object A of Count 1 of the
indictment: the felony offense of conspiracy to obtain money in the custody of a bank by false
pretenses.
5.
The North Carolina State Bar intends to file a formal complaint against Levinson
alleging he has violated the Rules of Professional Conduct based upon his felony convictions.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact, the undersigned makes the following:
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1.
The felony offenses for which Joseph Lee Levinson pled guilty is a serious crime
showing professional unfitness to practice law as defined by Rule .0103(17) of the State Bar
Discipline & Disability Rules and N.C. Gen. Stat. § 84-28(b).

2.
The undersigned is authorized, pursuant to Rule .0 lIS( d) of the State Bar
Discipline & Disability Rliles, to enter an order suspending an attorney's license upon receipt of
a certified copy of a plea of guilty to a criminal offense showing professional unfitness to
practice law.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, it is hereby
ORDERED:

1.
Joseph Lee Levinson's license to practice law in North Carolina is hereby
SUSPENDED until the conclusion of all disciplinary matters arising out of and related to the
above-referenced guilty plea in the United States District Court for the Middle District of North
Carolina. Pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code IB §§ .011S(d) and .0124(c), this Order shall be
effective 30 days from the date of service of this Order upon Levinson.
2.
Levinson shall comply with the wind down provisions contained in Rule .0124 of
the North Carolina State Bar Discipline and Disability Rules, 27 N.C. Admin. Code IE § .0124.
