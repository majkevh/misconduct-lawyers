ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B,
Rule. 0117 ofthe Discipline and Disability Rules ofthe North Carolina State Bar, upon
an affidavit of tender oflicense pursuant to Rule .01 17(a) executed by Mark Reid
Edmondson and filed in the offices ofthe North Carolina State Bar on July 5, 1996.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of Mark Reid
Edmondson is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2) in
that Mark Reid Edmondson misappropriated client funds in violation of Rules 1.2(b) and
(c) and 10. 1(a) and (c) of the North Carolina Bar Rules of Professional Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the
following order:
1. The tender of surrender of license of Mark Reid Edmondson is hereby
accepted.
2. Mark Reid Edmondson is hereby DISBARRED from the practice of law in
North Carolina.
3. Mark Reid Edmondson shall forthwith surrender his license and membership
card to the Secretary. He shall comply with all provisions of 27 N.C. Admin. Code,
Chapter 1, Rule .0124 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State
Bar.
4. The costs of this action are hereby taxed against Mark Reid Edmondson.
Done and ordered this the ~ day of July, 1996.
