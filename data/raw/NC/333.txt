ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT

THIS MATIER is before the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to a Petition for Reinstatement filed by William 1. Belk ("Belk") on February 15,2017.
Based upon the petition and a review of the records ofthe North Carolina State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT

I. On November 4, 2013 a hearing committee ofthe Disciplinary Hearing
Commission ("DHC") entered an Order of Discipline in 13 DHC 6 suspending Belk from
the practice of law for three years, effective December 12, 2013.
2. On February 15,2017, Belk filed a Petition for Reinstatement showing that he
had satisfied the conditions of the Order of Discipline.
3. Counsel for the North Carolina State Bar did not file an objection to Belk's
petition.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact, the Secretary makes the
following:
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1. The Petitioner, William 1. Belk, has satisfied the conditions necessary for
reinstatement that the original hearing committee included in its Order of Discipline.
2. The Petitioner has met the requirements set out in 27 NCAC 1B, ยง .0 125(b)
and the provisions ofthe November 4,2013 Order of Discipline. Petitioner is entitled to
reinstatement of his license to practice law in this jurisdiction.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, the
Secretary enters the following:

ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT

William I. Belk is hereby reinstated to the active practice oflaw in North Carolina
as of the date ofthis order.
