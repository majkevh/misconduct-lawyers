This matter coming on to be heard and being heard on July
21, 1995 before the Council of the North Carolina State Bar
pursuant to Title 27, N.C.A.C., Chapter 1, Subchapter B, section
.0117 upon the tender of surrender of license of Desiree W.
Crawford made by affidavit sworn to and subscribed on July 12,
1995 and filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on
July 12, 1995; and
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the
conduct of Desiree W. Crawford is grounds for discipline pursuant
to N.C.G.S. Sec. 84-28(b} (2) in that Desiree W. Crawford
misappropriated client funds in violation of Rules 1.2(b} & (c)
and 10.1(a} & (c) of the North Carolina Rules of Professional
Conduct found in Title 27, N.C.A.C., Chapter 2.
Wherefore, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council
enters the following Order:
1.	

The tender of surrender of license of Desiree W.
Crawford is hereby accepted.

2.	

Desiree W. Crawford is hereby DISBARRED from the
practice of law in North Carolina.

3.	

Desiree W. Crawford shall forthwith surrender her
license certificate and membership card to the
Secretary.

4.	

Desiree W. Crawford shall comply with the provisions of
Title 27, N.C.A.C., Chapter 1, Subchapter B, Section
.0124 within the time limits contained in that section.

5.	

Desiree W. Crawford is taxed with the costs of this
action as assessed by the Secretary.

Done	 and ordered this the
