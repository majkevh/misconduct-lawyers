ORDER OF DISBARMENT

TI-IIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter I, Subchapter B,
Rule. 0117 of the Discipline and Disability Rilles of the North Carolina State Bar, upon
an affidavit of tender of license pursuant to Rule .0117(a) executed by Judith E. Garmon
and filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on March 17, 1997.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of Judith E. Garmon
is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2) in that Judith E.
Garmon misappropriated client funds in violation of Rule 1.2(b) and (c), Rille 10.1 (a) and
(c), and Rule I0.2(e) and commingled her personal funds with client funds in violation of
Rule 10.1 (a) and (c) of the North Carolina State Bar Rules of Professional Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the
following order:
I. The tender of surrender of license of Judith E. Garmon is hereby accepted.
2. Judith E. Garmon is hereby DISBARRED from the practice oflaw in North
Carolina.
3. Judith E. Garmon shall forthwith surrender her license and membership card to
the Secretary. She shall comply with all provisions of 27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter I,
Rule .0124 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar.
4. The costs of this action are hereby taxed against Judith E. Garmon.
