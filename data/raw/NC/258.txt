ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B,
Rule. 0117 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar, upon
an affidavit of tender of license pursuant to Rule .0117(a) executed by John D.
McLemore and filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on September 15,
1998.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of John D. McLemore
is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2) in that John D.
McLemore misappropriated funds intended to pay title insurance premiums for clients
and funds belonging to his former law firm in violation of Rule 8.4(c) of the Revised
Rules of Professional Conduct of the North Carolina State Bar.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the
following order:
1. The tender of surrender of license of John D. McLemore is hereby accepted.
2. John D. McLemore is hereby DISBARRED from the practice oflaw in North
Carolina.
3. John D. McLemore shall forthwith surrender his license and membership card
to the Secretary. He shall comply with all provisions of 27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter
1, Rule .0124 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar.
4. The costs of this action are hereby taxed against John D. McLemore.
