ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT

GAREY M. BALLANCE, Attorney )

THIS MATTER is before the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to a Petition for Reinstatement filed by Garey M. Ballance ("Ballance") on November I,
2011.
Based upon the petition and a review of the records of the North Carolina State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1. On July 6, 2007, a hearing panel of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission
("DHC") entered an Order of Discipline in 05 DHC 48 ('"Order of Discipline")
suspending Ballance from the practice oflaw for five years. The effective date of the
Order of Discipline was November 3,2005.

2. On November 1,2011, Ballance filed a petition for reinstatement showing that
he had satisfied the conditions of the Order of Discipline, although he did not satisfy all
conditions on a timely basis.

3. Counsel for the North Carolina State Bar did not file an objection to Balance's
petition.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact. the Secretary makes the following
CONCLUSION OF LA W
I. The Petitioner, Garey M. Ballance, has satisfied the requirements set out

in 27 NCAC 1B, § .0125(b) and is entitled to reinstatement of his license to practice law
in this jurisdiction.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings ofFaet and Conclusion of Law, the
Secretary enters the following:
ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT
Garey M. Ballance is hereby reinstated to the active practice oflaw in North
Carolina as of the date of this order.
