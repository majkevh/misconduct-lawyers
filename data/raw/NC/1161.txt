ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT

v.

BENJAMIN S. SMALL, Attorney,
Defendant

This matter is before the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar (hereafter
"State Bar") pursuant to a Petition for Order of Reinstatement filed by Benjamin S. Small
(hereafter "Small") on 13 April 2015. Based upon a review of the records of the State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
L
On 1 June 2012, a hearing panel of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission
entered an Order of Discipline in file number II DHC 13 (hereafter "Order of
Discipline").
2.
The Order of Discipline suspended Small's license to practice law
North Carolina for a period of two years, effective 5 July 2012.
3.

III

The two year suspension of Small's law license ended on 5 July 2014.

4.
On 13 April 20 IS, Small filed a petition in which he stated that he had
complied with the requirements for reinstatement.
5.
On or about 5 May 2015, Small submitted a verification of his petition for
reinstatement.
6.
The counsel of the State Bar has not filed an objection to Small's
reinstatement petition pursuant to 27 N. C. Admin. Code IB § .0125.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact, the Secretary makes the following:
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
L

Small has satisfied the requirements set out in 27 N. C. Admin. Code IB §

.0125.
-I -

THEREFORE, pursuant to 27 N. C. Admin. Code 1B § .0125, it is
hereby ordered:
1.
Benjamin S. Small is hereby reinstated to the practice of law in
North Carolina.
2.
Benjamin S. Small's State Bar membership card and law license
shall be returned to him.
