ORDER OF DISBARMENT

)

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B,
Rule. 0117 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar, upon
an affidavit of tender of license pursuant to Rule .OII7(a) executed by John B. Gladden
and filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on October 2,2000.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of John B. Gladden is
grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2) in that John B.
Gladden misappropriated funds from his former law firm's trust account in violation of
Rille 1.2(b) and (c), Rule IO.I(a) and (c), and Rule IO.2(e) of the North Carolina State
Bar Rules of Professional Conduct and misappropriated funds from his former law firm's
operating account in violation of Rule 1.2(b) and (c) of the North Carolina State Bar
Rules of Professional Conduct and Rule 8.4(b) and (c) of the North Carolina State Bar
Revised Rules of Professional Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the
following order:
1. The tender of surrender of license of John B. Gladden is hereby accepted.
2. Jolm B. Gladden is hereby DISBARRED from the practice of law in North
Carolina.
3. John B. Gladden shall forthwith surrender his license and membership card to
the Secretary. He shall comply with all provisions of27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter I,
Rule .0124 of the Discipline and Disability Rules ofthe North Carolina State Bar.
4. The costs of this action are hereby taxed against John B. Gladden.
Done and ordered this the 20 th day of October, 2000.
