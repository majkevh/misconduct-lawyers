THIS MATIER coming before the undersigned Chair of The Disciplinary Hearing
Commission (DHC) pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code IB § .0117(d) upon an affidavit of
surrender of license executed by Donna Karen Cody ("Cody") dated September 22, 2016
and filed with the Chair of the DHC on September 27,2016.
Based upon the pleadings and the record, the undersigned makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
I.
The Defendant, Donna Karen Cody (hereafter "Cody") was licensed to
practice law on August 24, 1985.
2.
During all periods relevant hereto, Cody was actively engaged in the
practice oflaw in Robbinsville, Graham County, North Carolina.
3.

Cody served as Administrator ofthe Estate of Kenneth William Frye.

4.
Between August and September, 2013 Cody misappropriated at least
$12,300.00 from funds entrusted to her in her fiduciary capacity as identified above and
submitted a false accounting under oath to the Clerk of Court to which she attached
falsified bank statements.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact the undersigned makes the following
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
27 N.C. Admin. Code I B § .01 08 provides thatthe Chair ofthe DHC has
the power and duty to enter orders disbarring members by consent.
I.

2.
27 N.C. Admin. Code IB § .01l7(d) states thatthe Chair of the DHC will
accept the surrender and issue an order of disbarment upon determination that an affidavit

of surrender ·filed pursuant to this subsection meets the requirements set out in 27 N.C.
Admin. Code 1B § .01 17(a)(1 ), (2), (3), and (4).
3.
Defendant's affidavit meets all requirements set forth in 27 N.C. Admin.
Code IB §.0117(a)(I), (2), (3), (4) and (d) and the facts upon which the affidavit is
predicated warrant Defendant's disbarment.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, the
undersigned Chair of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission enters the following
ORDER
I.

The surrender of the license of Donna Karen Cody is hereby accepted.

2.
Donna Karen Cody is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North
Carolina effective upon the entry of this order with the Secretary of the North Carolina
State Bar.
3.
Donna Karen Cody shall comply with the provisions of27 N.C. Admin.
Code IB § .0117(c) and § .0124 of the State Bar Discipline and Disability Rules and shall
surrender her license and permanent membership card to the Secretary of the North
Carolina State Bar along with the affidavit required by 27 N.C. Admin. Code IB § .0124
of the State Bar Discipline and Disability Rules.
4.
Cody shall provide to the State Bar within thirty days of the effective date
of this Order an address and telephone number at which clients seeking return of their
files can communicate with Cody and obtain such files.
5. Cody shall return client files to the client within five days of receipt of a
request for return of the client file. Cody will be deemed to have received any such
request three days after the date the request is sent to her if the request is sent to the
address Cody provided to the State Bar pursuant to this Order.
6. Cody's law license shall not be reinstated until she has demonstrated to the
satisfaction of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission and the Council of the North
Carolina State Bar that she has complied with 27 N.C. Admin. Code 1B § .0125.
7. The costs of this action and administrative fees are taxed against Defendant.
Defendant shall pay the costs within sixty days of service of a statement of costs upon
her.
