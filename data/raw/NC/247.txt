ORDER OF DISBARMENT

This matter coming on to be heard and being heard on April 4, 1997 before the Council of
the North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C.A.C. IB, §.OlI7 upon the tender of surrender of
the license of George Wilborn Rives made by affidavit sworn to and subscribed on February 27,
1997 and filed in the office of the North Carolina State Bar on March 3, 1997; and
BASED UPON the contents of the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of George
Wilborn Rives is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C.G.S. § 84-28(b)(2) in that Rives
misappropriated clients' funds in violation of Rules 1.2(b) & (c) and IO.I(a) & (c) of the NC
Rules of Professional Conduct found in 27 N.C.A.C. 2.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the following
ORDER:
1.	

The tender of surrender of license of George Wilborn Rives is hereby accepted.

2.	

GEORGE WILBORN RIVES is hereby DISBARRED from the practice oflaw in
North Carolina.

3.	

George Wilborn Rives shall forthwith surrender his license certificate and
membership card to the Secretary.

4.	

George Wilborn Rives shall comply with the provisions of27 N.C.A.C. IB, §.OI24
within the time limits contained in that section.

5.	

George Wilborn Rives is taxed with the costs of this action as assessed by the
Secretary.

DONE AND ORDERED this the
