ORDER

This cause was heard by the undersigned Chairman of the Hearing Committee
of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission of the North Carolina State Bar on
motion of the plaintiff to dismiss the complaint filed against William T.
Combs, Jr. The plaintiff moved for a dismissal with prejudice in this action
due to the death of the defendant, William T. Combs, Jr., prior to the trial
of this actio~ It appears to the undersigned chairman that the plaintiff's
motion should be allowed.
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that the action captioned, The North Carolina
State Bar vs. William T. Combs, Jr. (86 DHC 10), is hereby dismissed with
prejudice.
