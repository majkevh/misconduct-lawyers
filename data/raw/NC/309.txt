ORDER OF

--------------------------~)-------------------------THIS MATTER is before the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to 27 N.C.A.C. 1B § .0125(b)(1) & (5), upon a Petition for Reinstatement filed by
Jeffrey R. Baker on 13 October 2016.
Based upon the petition and a review of the records of the North Carolina State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following
FINDINGS OF FACT

1.
On 5 October 2015, a hearing panel of the Disciplinary Hearing
Commission (DHC) entered an Order of Discipline in North Carolina State Bar v.
Jeffrey R. Baker, 15 DHC 14 (hereinafter "the Order").
2.
The Order suspended Baker's license to practice law for a period of one
year. The suspension of Baker's law license was effective 12 November 2015.
3.
On 13 October 2016, Baker submitted a petition seeking reinstatement of
his law license to active status.
4.
The Order set forth several requirements with which Baker was required
to comply in order to be eligible for reinstatement. In his verified petition, Baker
described the actions he has taken to comply with the requirements of the Order and
provided supporting documentation.

CONCLUSION OF LAW
The petitioner, Jeffrey R. Baker, has satisfied the conditions established in the
Order for reinstatement to active status.
Based upon the findings and conclusions stated herein, the Secretary enters the
following ORDER:
The petitioner, Jeffrey R. Baker, is reinstated to the active practice of law in
North Carolina as of the date of this order.
