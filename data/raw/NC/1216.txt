ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MA ITER came on to be heard and was heard by the undersigned Judge of the
Superior Court of Wake County. Based upon the Affidavit of Surrender of Law License
submitted to the court by Nicholas A. Stratas, Jr., the Court makes the following:

FINDINGS OF FACT

I. Nicholas A. Stratas, Jr. pled guilty to the following criminal offenses: Felony
Embezzlement, Felony Forgery (l2CRS4532), Felony Obstruction of Justice
(I 1CRS216881), Misdemeanor Larceny (11 CRS211653), Unauthorized Use of Motor
Vehicle (13CRS646).
2. Stratas acknowledged to the Court that he is in fact guilty of the criminal offenses to
which he pled guilty.

3. The State of North Carolina tendered an offer of proof reflecting that Stratas is in fact
guilty of the offenses to which he pled guilty.
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact, the court makes the following:
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

I. Stratas engaged in criminal conduct that reflects adversely on his professional fitness in
violation of North Carolina Rule of Professional Conduct 8A(b).
2. Stratas' misconduct constitutes grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section
84-28(b)(l).
IT IS NOW, THEREFORE, ORDERED:
I. Nicholas A. Stratas, Jr. is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North Carolina.
2. Stratas shall comply with the provisions ofSubchaptet B, Section .0124 of the Rules and
Regulations of the North Carolina State Bar and shall surrender his license and pennanent
