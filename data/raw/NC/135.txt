ORDER

THIS MAlTER coming before the undersigned Chair of The Disciplinary Hearing
Commission pursuant to 27 N.C.A.C. 1B 9.01 17(d) upon an affidavit of surrender of
license executed by Thomas P. Heller ("Heller") dated June 11 2008 and filed in the
ofices of the North Carolina State Bar on June 13, 2008.
Based upon the pleadings and the record, the undersigned makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1. Defendant was licensed to practice law in North Carolina in 1979.
2. Defendant's address of record with the North Carolina State Bar is P. 0. Box
816, Oak Island, NC 28465.
3. On October 19, 2007, the North Carolina State Bar filed a formal complaint
against Defendant alleging he violated the Rules of Professional Conduct based upon
his transfer of $17,046.00 from his trust accounts to his operating account, to which
fmds he has not been able to demonstrate entitlement. This conduct constitutes
grounds for discipline under N.C. Gen. Stat. 9 84-28 (b)(2), in that Heller violated Rules
1.15 through 1.15-3 and Rule 8.4(c) of the Revised Rules of Professional Conduct.
4. Defendant has indicated his wish to consent to disbarment by filing an
affidavit with the Disciplinary Hearing Commission. The affidavit meets all reql-~irements
set forth in 27 N.C.A.C. 1B 9.01 17(a)(l), (2), (3), (4) and (d).
Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact the undersigned makes the following

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1. 27 N.C.A.C. 1B s.0108 provides that the Chair has the power and duty to
enter orders disbarring members by consent.
2. Defendant's affidavit meets all requirements set forth in 27 N.C.A.C. 1B
ยง.0117(a)(l), (2), (3), (4) and (d) and the facts upon which the affidavit is predicated
warrant Defendant's disbarment.

Based upon the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, the
undersigned Chair of the Disciplinary Hearing Commission enters the following
ORDER
1. The surrender of the license of Thomas P. Heller is hereby accepted.
2. Thomas P. Heller is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North Carolina
effective upon the entry of this order with the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar.

3. Thomas P. Heller shall comply with the provisions of 27 N.C.A.C. l B 5.0124 of
the State Bar Discipline and Disability Rules and shall surrender his license and
permanent membership card to the Secretary of the North Carolina State Bar along with
the affidavit required by 27 N.C.A.C. 'IB s.0124 of the State Bar Discipline and Disability
Rules.
4. The costs of this action are taxed against the Defendant.
