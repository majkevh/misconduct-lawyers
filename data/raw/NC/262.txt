ORDER OF DISBARMENT

This matter came on to be heard by the Council of the North Carolina
State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B, Rule .0117
of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar, upon an
affidavit of tender of license pursuant to Rule .0117(a) executed by Rodney D.
Tigges and filed in the office of the North Carolina State Bar on May 6, 1999.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of Rodney D.
Tigges is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2)
in that Rodney D. Tigges misappropriated client funds in violation of Rule 8.4(b)
and (c) of the Revised Rules of Professional Conduct of the North Carolina State
Bar.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters
the following order:

1.	 The tender of surrender of license of Rodney D. Tigges is hereby
accepted.
2.	 Rodney D. Tigges is hereby DISBARRED from the practice of law in
North Carolina.
3.	 Rodney D. Tigges shall forthwith surrender his law license and
membership card to the Secretary. He shall comply with all provisions
of 27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter 1, Rule .0124 of the Discipline and
Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar.
4.	 The costs of this action are hereby taxed against Rodney D. Tigges.
Done and ordered this the 23 rd day of July, 1999.

AFFIDAVIT

3. I acknowledge that the facts forming the basis of the investigation or other
proceeding and referenced herein are true.
4. I submit my resignation because I know if charges were predicated upon the
misconduct under investigation, I could not successfully defend against them.
