THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B,
Rule. 0117 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar, upon
an affidavit of tender of license pursuant to Rule .0 117(a) executed by William G. Ijames
Ir. and filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on Iuly 6,2000.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of William G. Ij ames
Ir. is grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2) in that
William G. Ijames Jr. misappropriated estate and trust account funds in violation of Rule
1.15-1(a), Rule I.l5-2(h), Rule 8.4(b) and Rule 8.4(c) ofthe North Carolina State Bar
Revised Rules of Professional Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the
following order:
I. The tender of surrender of license of William G. Ijames Ir. is accepted.
2. William G. Ijames Ir. is DISBARRED from the practice of law in North
Carolina.
3. William G. Ijames Jr. shall forthwith surrender his license and membership
card to the Secretary. He shall comply with all provisions 01'27 N.C. Admin. Code,
Chapter I, Rule .0124 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State
Bar.
4. The costs of this action are hereby taxed against William G. Ijames Jr.
Done and ordered this the 21 st day of July, 2000.

AFFIDAVIT

To: The Council of the North Carolina State Bar
1. I, William G. Ijames Jr., hereby tender my law license and desire to resign
from the North Carolina State Bar. My resignation is freely and voluntarily rendered. It
is not the result of coercion or duress. I am fully aware of the implications of submitting
my resignation.
2. I am aware that there is pending an investigation or other proceeding regarding
allegations that I misappropriated funds from the Henry Weir White estate and my trust
account.
3. I acknowledge that the material facts forming the basis of the investigation or
other proceeding and referenced herein in this affidavit are true.
4. I submit my resignation because I know if charges were predicated upon the
misconduct under investigation, I could not successfully defend against them.
