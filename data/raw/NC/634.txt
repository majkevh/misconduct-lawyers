THIS MATTER is before the Secretary ofthe North Carolina State Bar pursuant
to a Petition for Order Staying Suspension filed by Jason Alfred Matthew Gold ("Gold")
on August 27,2015.
Based upon the petition and a review of the records of the North Carolina State
Bar, the Secretary makes the following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1. On September 26, 2013, a hearing panel of the Disciplinary Hearing
Commission ("DHC") entered an Order of Discipline in 12 DHC 34 ("Order of
Discipline") suspending Gold from the practice oflaw for five years effective on the date
of the Order of Discipline. The Order of Discipline provided that, after serving two years
of active suspension, Gold could apply for a stay of the balance of the suspension ifhe
satisfied certain conditions.
2. On August 27, 2015, Gold filed a petition for reinstatement showing that he
had satisfied the conditions set forth in the Order of Discipline for him to be eligible for a
stay and the conditions set fOlih in 27 NCAC IB, ยง .0125(b) to be reinstated.
3. Counsel for the North Carolina State Bar did not file an objection to Gold's
petition.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact, the Secretary makes the
following:

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1. The Petitioner, Jason Alfred Matthew Gold, has satisfied the requirements set
out in his Order of Discipline to have the balance of his suspension stayed; and
2. Jason Alfred Matthew Gold has satisfied the requirements of27 NCAC lE, ยง
.0125(b) to be entitled to reinstatement of his license to practice law in this jurisdiction.
BASED UPON the foregoing Findings of Fact and Conclusion of Law, the
Secretary enters the following:
ORDER STAYING SUSPENSION
The balance of Jason Alfred Matthew Gold's suspension is hereby stayed and
Jason Alfred Matthew Gold is reinstated to the active practice oflaw in North Carolina as
of the date of this order.
