RODDEY M. BROWN III

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council of the North Carolina State Bar
pursuant to Section .0117 of Subchapter B of the Rules and Regulations of the North Carolina State Bar upon an
affidavit of tender of license executed by Roddey M. Brown III and filed in the offices of the North Carolina
State Bar on October 21, 2009.
Based upon the affidavit of surrender of law license, the Council finds that Roddey M. Brown 111
misappropriated $21,282.16 entrusted to him in a fiduciary capacity and utilized those funds for his own personal
benefit without authorization to do so from the beneficial owners of the funds.
The Council also finds that Roddey M. Brown III has waived his right to notice of this hearing and the right to be
heard before the Council.
The Council concludes that the conduct of Roddey M. Brown III constitutes grounds for discipline under N.C.
Gen. Stat. §84-28(b)(2) in that Roddey M. Brown III violated Rules l.IS-2UJ and 8.4(b) and (c) of the North
Carolina Rules of Professional Conduct and that the Council may enter an order of discipline accordingly.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council of the North Carolina State Bar enters the
following order:
1. The tender of surrender of license of Roddey M. Brown III is hereby accepted.
2. Roddey M. Brown III is hereby DISBARRED from the practice of law in North Carolina.
3. Roddey M. Brown III will comply with the provisions of Subchapter B, Section .0124 of the Rules and
Regulations of the North Carolina State Bar. Brown will surrender his license and pennanent registration card to
the Secretary ofthe North Carolina State Bar along with the affidavit required by Subchapter B, Section .0124.
4. The costs of this action are taxed against Roddey M. Brown III.
