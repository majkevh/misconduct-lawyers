THIS MATTER came on to be heard and was heard by the undersigned Executive
Director of the N.C. State Bar pursuant to the motion of the Office of Counsel to dismiss
this matter for mootness. Based upon the pleadings filed herein the Director makes the
following:
FINDINGS OF FACT
1. On or about May 25, 2005, Deborah Church signed an affidavit tendering the
surrender of her license to the Council of the N.C. State Bar.
2. Church's affidavit of surrender was filed with the N. C. State Bar on May 26,
2005.
3. On July 1,2005, before Church's affidavit of surrender could be heard by the
State Bar Council, the Wake County Superior Court entered an order disbarring Ms.
Church from the practice of law in North Carolina.
4. There are no further pleadings or issues pending before the Council in this
matter.
Based on the Findings of Fact, the Director makes the following:
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
1. Ms. Church's affidavit of surrender in 05 BCS 4 is now moot, all issues
concerning her law license having been finally determined by the Wake County Superior
Court.
2. This matter should be closed.

WHEREFORE IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the State Bar's file in 05 BCS 4
is hereby closed.
