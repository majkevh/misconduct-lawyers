ORDER OF DISBARMENT

THIS MATTER coming on to be heard and being heard by the Council ofthe
North Carolina State Bar pursuant to 27 N.C. Admin. Code Chapter 1, Subchapter B,
Rule. 0117 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar, upon
an affidavit of tender of license pursuant to Rule .0117(a) executed by James T. Lee and
filed in the offices of the North Carolina State Bar on October 19, 1999.
Based upon the affidavit, the Council finds that the conduct of James T. Lee is
grounds for discipline pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. Section 84-28(b)(2) in that James T.
Lee misappropriated client funds in violation of Rule 1.15-1 (a) and (d) and Rule 1.15Â­
2(g) and (h) of the North Carolina State Bar Revised Rules of Professional Conduct.
WHEREFORE, upon motion duly made and seconded, the Council enters the
following order:
1. The tender of surrender of license of James T. Lee is hereby accepted.
2. James T. Lee is hereby DISBARRED from the practice oflaw in North
Carolina.
3. James T. Lee shall forthwith surrender his license and membership card to the
Secretary. He shall comply with all provisions of 27 N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter 1, Rule
.0124 of the Discipline and Disability Rules of the North Carolina State Bar.
4. The costs of this action are hereby taxed against James T. Lee.

Done and ordered this the 22nd day of October, 1999.


Robert C. Sink, President
The North Carolina State Bar
