THOMAS, CARDELL A.
90-DB-033

REPRIMAND 

DISCIPLINARY BOARD
LOUISIANA STATE BAR ASSOCIATION
NO: 90-DB-033
IN RE: CARDELL A. THOMAS

               OPINION OF THE DISCIPLINARY BOARD

     The Disciplinary Board has considered the request for approval of "Discipline by Consent" as provided by Section 20 of Rule XIX.  The Disciplinary Board approves the consent discipline as requested and as set forth in the record, and, accordingly, it is ordered that Cardell A. Thomas be and he is hereby publicly reprimanded for his conduct set forth in these proceedings. it is ordered that a formal public reprimand be issued to the respondent.

     
                              By: Gerard F. Thomas, Jr.

                              For the Disciplinary Board

                         

Dissents:

     I dissent to the imposition of two separate public reprimands for the two offenses found in 90-PDB-004 and 90-DB-033.  It is my belief that a single public reprimand ought to be entered for both offenses.

                              Trevor G. Bryan

I join in the dissent for the reasons stated.

                              Harvey  Lewis
