IN RE: O'KEEFE, MICHAEL H.
1983-1200 (La.12/7/1984); ___ So.2d ___

SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA

NO. 83-B-1200

LOUISIANA STATE BAR ASSOCIATION VS. MICHAEL H. O'KEEFE

 

ORDER

PREMISES CONSIDERED,

IT IS ORDERED that respondent be allowed to voluntarily surrender his license to practice law,  pursuant to Article XV, Section 11 of the Articles of Incorporation, Louisiana State Bar Association, consensual disbarment, effective December 22, 1993.

DATED AND SIGNED at New Orleans, Louisiana, this 7th day of December, 1984.
