EDWARDS, EDWIN W.
2003-0722 (La. 04/01/2003) 843 So.2d 1065 Permanent Resignation in lieu of Discipline

SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA

NO. 03-OB-0722

IN RE: EDWIN W. EDWARDS

ORDER

Considering the Petition for Voluntary Permanent Resignation from the Practice of Law filed by Edwin W. Edwards, and the concurrence thereto filed by the Office of Disciplinary Counsel,

IT IS ORDERED that the request of Edwin W. Edwards for permanent resignation in lieu of discipline be and is hereby granted, pursuant to Supreme Court Rule XIX, § 20.1.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that Edwin W. Edwards shall be permanently prohibited from practicing law in Louisiana or in any other jurisdiction in which he is admitted to the practice of law; shall be permanently prohibited from seeking readmission to the practice of law in this state or in any other jurisdiction in which he is admitted; and shall be permanently prohibited from seeking admission to the practice of law in any jurisdiction.

NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA, this _____ day of April, 2003.
