LEVY, RUSSELL DAVID
2003-1031 (La. 05/27/2003) 848 So.2d 516 Permanent Resignation

SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA

NO. 03-OB-1031

IN RE: RUSSELL DAVID LEVY

ORDER

Considering the Petition for Voluntary Permanent Resignation from the Practice of Law filed by Russell David Levy, Louisiana Bar Roll number 23168, and the concurrence thereto filed by the Office of Disciplinary Counsel,

IT IS ORDERED that the request of Russell David Levy for permanent resignation in lieu of discipline be and is hereby granted, pursuant to Supreme Court Rule XIX, § 20.1.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that Russell David Levy shall be permanently prohibited from practicing law in Louisiana or in any other jurisdiction in which he is admitted to the practice of law; shall be permanently prohibited from seeking readmission to the practice of law in this state or in any other jurisdiction in which he is admitted; and shall be permanently prohibited from seeking admission to the practice of law in any jurisdiction.

NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA, this _____ day of May, 2003.
