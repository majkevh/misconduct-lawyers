MOSLEY, DEAN M.
92-2491 (La. 10/19/92); 605 So. 2d 1154

   

Supreme Court of Louisiana
No. 92-B-2491
In Re: Dean M. Mosley

Disciplinary Proceedings

Per Curiam.

*************************************************************************************

     Upon review of the record of the disciplinary board's findings and recommendations, and the record filed herein, it is the decision of the court that the disciplinary board's recommendations be adopted.

     Accordingly, it is ordered that Dean M. Mosley be and he is hereby suspended from the practice of law for a period of one year and one day from the finality of this decision and that he pay all costs.

SUSPENSION ORDERED.
