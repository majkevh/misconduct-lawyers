BARNETT, JACK D.
1991-1562 (La. 11/21/1991); 589 So. 2d 2 90-DB-024 Neglect of Legal Matters Suspension

SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA

NO. 91 B 1562

IN RE: JACK D. BARNETT

DISCIPLINARY PROCEEDING

PER CURIAM

After reviewing the findings and recommendations of the Hearing Committee and the Disciplinary Board; transcript, record, and briefs filed; as well as the arguments by the parties; it is ordered that Jack D. Barnett be suspended from the practice of law for a period of one year.

All costs of this proceeding are assessed to Barnett.

SUSPENSION ORDERED
